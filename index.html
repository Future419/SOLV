What does the Solv supply chain entail?

Master Anchor

Master Anchors are responsible for the production and supply of goods to the MSME sector. They often produce goods in significant quantities and may have multiple product lines.

Anchor

Anchors play a crucial role in the supply chain by ensuring that products from Master Anchors reach the MSMEs efficiently. They may offer services such as bulk purchasing and logistics.

Dealer

Dealers operate at the retail level and interact directly with end customers. They purchase products from Anchors or other distributors and provide them to customers in smaller quantities.

Program

A program represents a formal agreement or set of terms and conditions established between SOLV and financial institutions. These terms outline the specific parameters and criteria.
